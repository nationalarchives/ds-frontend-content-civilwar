What kind of king was Charles I?

Case Study 2: Charles I as a ruler

The rulers who came before Charles I were Elizabeth I (1558-1603) and James I (1603-25).

In this case study you can examine a range of sources to see whether Charles I carried on ruling in the same style or whether he brought in changes.

Sources:

2. Documents on the Duke of Buckingham, 1627
3. Order to pay a bill for royal gowns, 1627
