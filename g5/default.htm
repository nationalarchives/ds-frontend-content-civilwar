Why did Britain become a republic?

The period 1647-53 was a very significant time in British history. Charles I was finally defeated. In 1649 he was put to death, leaving the country a republic, without a monarch to lead it.

This document is one of many that give us clues about those times. A document like this would have left most people speechless. Have a closer look at it and the other original sources.
