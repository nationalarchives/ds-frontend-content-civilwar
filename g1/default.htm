Sensational Sources

The Civil War was an amazing time. England, Scotland, Ireland and Wales were all plunged into war and rebellion. Parliament fought against the King in two wars. A revolution followed and the King was executed. England became a republic. After that Oliver Cromwell came to power.

Any period with all that going on must produce some interesting historical documents (like this secret code).
